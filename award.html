AWARDS

Discover the accolades and recognition that Priya Shah has received for her outstanding contributions to matchmaking. This page showcases the numerous honors and awards that she has received, recognizing her dedication, expertise, and outstanding service in creating successful matches.

Youngest Jain Ratna Award, 2010
Priya Shah was overwhelmed when she received the Youngest Jain Ratna Award.
Nari Ratna Award, 2011
Priya Shah, the matchmaker, received the "Nari Ratna" Award on International Women’s Day, March 8, 2011. The award was presented by Varsha Gaikwad (Hon. Ex Minister for Women & Child Development, Govt. of Maharashtra) and Shri Deepchand Gardi (President of the All India Jain Federation).
Mother Teressa Excellence Award & Priyadarshini Indira Gandhi Award, 2013
Priya Shah, the Matchmaker, received the Mother Teresa Excellence Award in 2013 and the Priyadarshini Indira Gandhi Award in 2013. These accolades were given by the International Integrity Peace & Friendship Society in recognition of her outstanding services to society in the field of matrimony.
Ashok Stambh Award, 2014
Priya Shah, the Matchmaker, received the Ashok Stambh Award in 2014 from the Lions Club, Mumbai.
Global Achiever’s Award, 2015
Priya Shah was awarded the Global Achiever’s Award - 21st Century - The Best Matchmaker Award - 2015, in Dubai.
Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Excellence Award
Priya Shah was honored with the ‘Bharat Ratna Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Excellence Award, 2018, by Association for Rising and Talented Personalities..
Times of India Inspiring Entrepreneurs Award, 2020
Priya Shah, the young celebrity Matchmaker, was honored with the Times of India Inspiring Entrepreneurs Award in 2020.
International Iconic Award, 2021
Priya Shah, the Matchmaker, received the "International Iconic Award" at the Sahara Star Hotel in August 2021.
Global Achievers Awards, 2021
HBW News, an international digital media platform, announced the winners of the Global Achievers Awards 2021. Priya Shah, the Matchmaker, was recognized for her outstanding achievement in the matchmaking service sector and received the award for Best Wedding Matchmaker.
Shakti Award - Most Powerful Top 20 Women in India
Dr. Priya Shah, the Matchmaker, received the "Most Powerful Top 20 Women in India" Award from the Government of India. The award was presented by Smt. Smriti Irani, Union Minister.
Golden Glory Award, 2023
Pareeniti Chopra presented Priya Shah with the "Best Matchmaker of the Year 2023" award at the Golden Glory Awards - Bollywood Edition, held at The Leela Palace Hotel, Mumbai.
WASME SME Excellence Award, 2021
Dr. Priya Shah was awarded as the Best Matchmaker in India by the World Association for Small & Medium Enterprises and the Ministry of MSME, Government of India.
Times Power Women 2020
Priya Shah awarded Times Power Women 2020, along with other celebrated women.
JITO
Jain International Trade Organization honours Priya Shah, the Matchmaker.
