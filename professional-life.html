Professional Life

Dr. Priya Shah, M.A. & Ph.D. in Marriage Consultancy (U.S.A.), has been running a marriage bureau in Mumbai, India, and abroad since 1991 under the establishment ‘Arrange Love Marriage’ an international marriage bureau, with office branches in Mumbai and New York. Over the past 30 years, she has built immense trust and goodwill and is proud to announce that ‘Arrange Love Marriage’ has successfully matched over 9000 couples, helping them find their ideal partners.

Priya Shah dedicates significant time to communicating with brides, grooms, and their respective families to ensure they are introduced to genuine, compatible families.

Her clients are highly qualified professionals, including doctors, engineers, MBAs, chartered accountants, architects, and well-known business owners. They come from diverse Hindu backgrounds, such as Gujarati, Punjabi, Sindhi, Bengali, South Indian, Marwari-Agarwal, Maheshwari, Gupta, Jain, and more. These clients are well-established in Mumbai, India's metro cities, and overseas in countries like the USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia, and Africa. They span various age groups, starting from 1980 to 2003 and beyond.

Arrange Love Marriage, continuously evolves with the times. In an era where online chatting and rushed marriage proposals can lead to misunderstandings or divorce, it is crucial for individuals to choose the right partner. Marriage should bring happiness and mutual care under all circumstances, and Priya Shah is dedicated to raising awareness for a healthy and happy married life. To further this goal, she introduced the magazine “The Marriages of India,” which she hopes will guide young people towards a joyful married life.

Priya Shah Works On Your Dreams

As India's pioneering and unique matchmaker, Dr. Priya Shah treats her clients like family. She provides comprehensive information about potential matches, including family background, personal qualities, nature, behavior, culture, and upbringing. Unlike other matchmakers, she goes beyond mere bio-data and photos, offering in-depth insights into each candidate and their family.

Dr. Shah believes in personal interaction and handles all her clients personally, without delegating to her team. She often says, “A top surgeon performs the operation themselves and does not rely on their support staff." Similarly, she caters to all her clients' needs personally, ensuring a meticulous and dedicated approach.

Her sole goal is to work tirelessly to find the most compatible matches, bringing people together to form beautiful unions. Nothing brings her more happiness and satisfaction than adding new families to her life through successful matches. She is committed to openness and honesty in her client relationships. She doesn't just share profiles and photos; she deeply researches every family and prospective match, providing the most in-depth and honest reports. She maintains constant communication with clients and their families to understand their nature and culture, ensuring the right information and narrative about each match.

Marriage is a significant life decision, and finding the perfect partner requires attention and care. Dr. Priya Shah is dedicated to ensuring that this journey is as smooth and successful as possible.
